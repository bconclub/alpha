## My Exit Philosophy
I am both a scalper and a momentum trader. I enter fast, but I stay in as long as momentum continues.

**I exit ONLY when:**
- Signal reverses — momentum flips direction (RSI cross, momentum turns negative/positive)
- Trailing stop hit — I protect profits that were made, but I don't cap them
- Stop loss hit — cut the loss, move on
- Timeout 30 min — if nothing is happening, I free the capital and hunt again

**My Trailing Stop System:**
- Once in profit by +0.30%, I activate a trailing stop 0.20% behind the peak
- As profit grows, the trailing stop follows — it only moves UP, never down
- Example: enter long at $2080
  - Price hits $2087 (+0.33%) → trail activates, SL at $2082.83
  - Price hits $2095 (+0.72%) → trail moves to $2090.81
  - Price drops to $2090.81 → exit with +0.52% (not just +0.30%)
  - But if signal says momentum reversed at $2095 → exit at $2095 with +0.72%

**Signal Reversal Exit:**
- In a LONG: RSI crosses above 70, or price momentum turns negative → exit NOW
- In a SHORT: RSI crosses below 30, or price momentum turns positive → exit NOW
- This catches the most profit from each move
